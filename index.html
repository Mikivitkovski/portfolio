Hey there, I’m Miki Vitkovski, a junior web developer passionate about crafting immersive digital experiences through innovative web solutions. Bringing ideas to life through captivating user interfaces is my forte. I specialize in leveraging the power of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript frameworks such as Angular to build intuitive, responsive, and visually stunning front-end designs. The magic behind the scenes – that's where I thrive too.
